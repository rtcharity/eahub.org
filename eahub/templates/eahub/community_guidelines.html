Community Guidelines

Our mission is to facilitate connections and collaborations between community members. We want the Hub to be a safe and welcoming space to allow this to happen, so we request that you follow the following community guidelines:

  • Be kind, respectful and courteous
  • Avoid advertising services and products on your profile or via messaging, unless it is explicitly related to effective altruism.

We do not tolerate discrimination, threats of violence, harassment, misinformation, disinformation, or other things which may harm users.

We will take action if these guidelines are violated. Depending on the situation, we may give a warning or suspend the individual temporarily or permanently.

Please report behavior that goes against our community guidelines by emailing contact@eahub.org or click the ‘Report an Issue’ button on a user’s profile.
