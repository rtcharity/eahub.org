About

The Effective Altruism Hub is here to make it easier for Effective Altruists to get together and collaborate on shared goals. Our vision is to increase visibility for individuals, projects and groups, and to make ideas and opportunities easier to find.

The Hub allows you to create, share or find personal and group profiles. Users can search for volunteers, speakers, and talent. You can also find, join or start local groups. Each of these entities are helpfully displayed on the interactive map of EAs. Improved privacy settings allow users to customise how they use the Hub.

In the near future we will be working on introducing more functionality.
